What is STRIDE Threat Modelling?

STRIDE is a threat modelling methodology developed by Microsoft that systematically identifies threats across six categories: Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, and Elevation of Privilege. CVEPulse applies STRIDE analysis to your application architecture, data flows, and trust boundaries to identify security weaknesses before they become vulnerabilities.

What differentiates our approach is the direct translation of identified threats into actionable SIEM detection use cases. Each threat model deliverable includes custom detection rules for your SIEM platform ‚Äî whether that's Microsoft Sentinel KQL queries, Splunk SPL searches, or QRadar rules ‚Äî ensuring your security monitoring covers the specific attack vectors relevant to your application.
