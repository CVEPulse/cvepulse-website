Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

CVEPulse collects information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms, sign-up forms, and dashboard registration. This may include your name, email address, company name, phone number, and service interests. We also collect standard web analytics data including page views, browser type, and referral sources.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, provide requested services, send threat intelligence alerts (if subscribed), improve our platform, and communicate about our services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Sources

Our threat intelligence dashboards aggregate publicly available cybersecurity data from sources including CISA KEV, NVD, and Ransomware.live. This data is publicly available and does not contain personal information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

Cookies

CVEPulse uses essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
