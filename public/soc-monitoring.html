SOC Monitoring FAQ

What is SOC as a Service (SOCaaS)?
SOC as a Service provides outsourced security operations center capabilities including 24/7 monitoring, alert triage, incident response, and threat hunting ‚Äî without the cost of building and staffing an in-house SOC. CVEPulse delivers SOCaaS powered by real-time threat intelligence and experienced security analysts.
What is the difference between MDR and traditional SOC monitoring?
Traditional SOC monitoring focuses on alert monitoring and escalation. Managed Detection and Response (MDR) goes further by including active threat hunting, automated and manual response actions, containment, and remediation guidance. CVEPulse MDR combines detection technology with human expertise for comprehensive protection.
Which SIEM platforms does CVEPulse manage?
CVEPulse provides SIEM management services for Microsoft Sentinel, Splunk Enterprise/Cloud, IBM QRadar, Elastic Security, LogRhythm, and other platforms. Services include deployment, log source onboarding, detection rule development, performance tuning, and ongoing operational management.
